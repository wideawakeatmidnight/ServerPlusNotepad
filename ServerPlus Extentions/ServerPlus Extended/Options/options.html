Objectives

ServerPlus Notepad was developed for experienced ServerPlus Technicians. It is meant to solve the common problems and ineffiecines that are associated with taking notes and interacting with Tracker during a call.

Efficient and Accurate Notetaking

Taking good notes is critical to our occupation. Our notes are our primary expression to ServerPlus clients that we are effectively performing our job. This is why escalated tickets are evaluated so scrupulously; these tickets are guaranteed to be viewed by the company. Their Tier 2 department is reliant on the information that we provide to adequately perform their function. And we can all appreciate when we open up a subscriber's profile and discover detailed notes that assist us in determining the most effect approach to helping them on them resolve their issues.

The problem lies in the fact that taking detailed notes can be very time consuming. The necessary information that we are required to consolidate and present is diverse and scattered. Collecting it requires patience, time and persistence. However, the abundance of time is something that we don’t always have at our disposal. The customer and our employer wants the issue resolved as quickly as possible. Other customers are waiting for our assistance. Completing the necessary tasks involved in extensive note taking while while entertaining a consumer and with a clock dictating that you should have resolved their issue earlier can be irritating and stressful. Our desire to complete an issue as quickly as possible can result in poor note taking.

ServerPlus Notepad purpose is to assist with balancing these two occasionally conflicting requirements. The goal is to increase note taking accuracy while simultaneously reducing the amount of time it takes to compose these notes.

Sir James Paul McCartney, (born 18 June 1942), is an English musician, singer, songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, and composer. With John Lennon, George Harrison and Ringo Starr, he gained worldwide fame as a member of the Beatles, widely regarded as one of the most popular and influential acts in the history of rock music; his songwriting partnership with Lennon is one of the most celebrated of the 20th century. After the band's break-up, he pursued a solo career and later formed Wings with his first wife, Linda, and Denny Laine.

Inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame as a solo artist in March 1999, McCartney has written, or co-written 32 songs that have reached number one on the Billboard Hot 100, and as of 2014 he has sold more than 15.5 million RIAA-certified units in the United States. McCartney, Lennon, Harrison and Starr received MBEs in 1965, and in 1997, McCartney was knighted for his services to music.
